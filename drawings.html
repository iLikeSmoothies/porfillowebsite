Engineering Drawings

Drawing PDFs and preview images can live inside project folders under assets/projects/ or in assets/drawings/ when they are not tied to a specific project.

Drawings connect to project pages as work is documented.

Add project drawings in assets/projects/Project_Name/ and list them in data/projects.json. General drawing files can be stored in assets/drawings/.
